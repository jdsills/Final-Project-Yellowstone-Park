The Founding of Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone was founded on March 1, 1872, making it the world's first national park. President Ulysses S. Grant signed the Yellowstone National Park Protection Act into law, it protected more than 2 million acres of mountain wilderness, full of geologic activity and native wildlife.
